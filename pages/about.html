About Us

Who we are

Since 1979, we have been committed to delivering accurate and reliable results you can trust. Our laboratory provides high-quality diagnostic services for both humans and pets, combining experience, technology, and care. We understand that every test matters. That’s why our team works with precision and efficiency to support doctors, patients, and pet owners with timely results and dependable analysis. Our services include a wide range of laboratory exams, from routine tests to specialized diagnostics, always following strict quality standards. At the heart of what we do is a simple mission: to care for health—human and animal—with accuracy, responsibility, and compassion.
